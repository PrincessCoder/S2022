Exercise 1

Proof of Concept
This is a sample of the p5.js Riso library halftone application in fluorescent pink.

Building on the mid-century sketches completed in the Methods of Creativity Workshop, I am proposing a one point of interaction that randomly generates patterns by tapping a key on the keyboard.

Project 1: I would like to use this technique to create work on the wicked problem of sustainability in the mid-century modern style of illustration incorporating the p5.js Riso library to give the art a Risograph printed appearance.

This work is inspired by Bruce Mau's book "MAU MC24" specifically, the principle "We are not separate from or above nature" and the digital brushes and textures created by Retro Supply.
